This report maps Common Criteria and SSCD PP clauses to one concrete local-signing TOE: Samsung Galaxy S25 Ultra (model SM-S938), with clause-by-clause component mapping and linked authority references.

Status of This Document

This document is an unofficial draft report, dated February 20, 2026. It is not a specification, not a standards-track draft, and has no official standing.

This document is not a legal finding of qualified status. Legal qualified status requires the certification and listing path under eIDAS Article 29, Article 30, and Article 31 [[EIDAS910]].

Clause-level mapping in this report uses publicly downloadable SSCD PP documents (Part 2 and Part 5), CC Part 2/3, and CEM; EN 419 241 is included as migration-target reference via CEN-CENELEC search [[CENSEARCH]].

AI-assistance transparency: this report was produced with assistance from GPT-5.3-Codex under human editorial control. Implementation references for this edition include Samsung Galaxy S25 Ultra and Samsung Knox technical documentation [[SAMSUNGS25US]] [[SAMSUNGNEWS25]] [[KNOXVAULT]] [[KNOXTIMA]].

SAR/EAL Mapping for “Ready to Apply”

PP Part 2 and Part 5 both target EAL4 augmented with AVA_VAN.5 [[PPSSCD2]] [[PPSSCD5]]. The outcome is “methodically designed, tested, and reviewed,” plus advanced vulnerability analysis [[CCPART3]]. For the Galaxy S25 Ultra TOE, these assurance blocks still require a product ST and evaluator evidence package.

Assurance block What must exist
ASE_* (ST evaluation) Complete ST with conformance claims, objectives, requirements, and TOE summary specification.
ADV_* Architecture, functional spec, implementation representation, and modular design evidence.
ALC_* Lifecycle, configuration management, delivery, and development security evidence.
ATE_* Coverage, design-based tests, and independent testing samples.
AVA_VAN.5 Advanced methodical vulnerability analysis and penetration testing.

CEM work units and verdict rules are used to produce pass/fail evidence per assurance component [[CEMR5]].

Bottom Line

Samsung Galaxy S25 Ultra can be profiled toward local-QSCD behavior if the TOE boundary is strict around Knox Vault/keystore components, keys stay hardware-bound, trusted channels are explicit, and ST/SAR evidence is complete.
